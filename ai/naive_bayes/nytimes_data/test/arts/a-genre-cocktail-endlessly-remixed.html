http://www.nytimes.com/2014/10/26/arts/music/a-genre-cocktail-endlessly-remixed.html 2014-10-26 05:57:22 A Genre Cocktail, Endlessly Remixed SBTRKT, the recording project of the English songwriter, producer, D.J. and remixer Aaron Jerome, deals in tension and space, melancholy and jitters. === SBTRKT Onstage, in his nest of equipment with a shifting cast of backup musicians, SBTRKT plays drums and triggers instrumental and vocal samples in an improvisational remix of his songs; a concert veers from dance party to strobe-lighted meditation. “Impermanence is so permanently with me,” say the lyrics in the title song from his new album, “Wonder Where We Land,” as good an explanation of his music as any. (9 p.m. Sunday and Monday, Terminal 5, 610 West 56th Street, Clinton; 212-582-6600,
