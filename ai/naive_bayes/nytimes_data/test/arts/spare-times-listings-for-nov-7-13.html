http://www.nytimes.com/2014/11/07/arts/spare-times-listings-for-nov-7-13.html 2014-11-07 01:42:59 Spare Times Listings for Nov. 7-13 A selected guide to readings, exhibitions, walking tours and other cultural events in New York City. === Veterans Day Parade and Events Events begin with a ceremony at 10 a.m. at Madison Square Park, Fifth Avenue and 23rd Street. The parade starts at 11:11 a.m — in keeping with tradition, ceremonies are scheduled on the 11th day of the 11th month at the 11th hour. Marchers and marching bands from around the country will travel up Fifth Avenue, from 26th Street to 52nd Street. More details: Other events include a free ceremony at 3:30 p.m., with the placement of a wreath in the Hudson River, at Pier 86, at the Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum, 46th Street and 12th Avenue, Clinton. Other activities are planned throughout the day; museum admission is waived for veterans all day.
