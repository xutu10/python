Goffin next plays Jan-Lennard Struff, who won when third-seeded Philipp Kohlschreiber retired when trailing, 5-0. Second-seeded Gaël Monfils also advanced, hitting 12 aces in a 6-3, 6-4 win over seventh-seeded Jerzy Janowicz. He faces No. 6 João Sousa, who beat Paul-Henri Mathieu, 3-6, 6-3, 7-6 (4). ■ The Davis Cup final between France and Roger Federer’s Switzerland will be held at the Pierre Mauroy stadium in Lille, France. France, third on the list of Davis Cup winners behind Australia and the United States, is bidding for a 10th title. Switzerland is looking for its first.
