Milos Raonic lost to Ricardas Berankis, 6-3, 4-6, 6-3, and Dominika Cibulkova fell to Vitalia Diatchenko, 4-6, 6-2, 6-2. ■ Top seeded Tomas Berdych beat Dustin Brown, 7-5, 6-3, to reach the quarterfinals of the Stockholm Open. Berdych will face Marius Copil, who beat Pierre-Hugues Herbert, 6-4, 6-3.
