http://www.nytimes.com/2014/12/29/business/treasury-auctions-for-the-week-of-dec-29.html 2014-12-29 00:56:40 Treasury Auctions for the Week of Dec. 29 The following tax-exempt fixed-income issues are scheduled for pricing this week. === The Treasury’s schedule of financing this week includes Monday’s regular weekly auction of new three- and six-month bills and an auction of four-week bills on Tuesday. At the close of the New York cash market on Friday, the rate on the outstanding three-month bill was 0.01 percent. The rate on the six-month issue was 0.10 percent, and the rate on the four-week issue was 0.02 percent. No tax-exempt fixed-income issues are scheduled for pricing this week.
