http://www.nytimes.com/2014/10/18/sports/apprentice-jockeys-death-an-unfortunate-accident.html 2014-10-18 06:14:36 Apprentice Jockey’s Death ‘an Unfortunate Accident’ Investigators have found no wrongdoing by jockeys in this week’s death of a 17-year-old apprentice jockey riding at an Indiana horse track. === Investigators have found no wrongdoing by jockeys in this week’s death of Juan Saez, 17, an apprentice jockey, at Indiana Grand Racing & Casino in Shelbyville. The Indiana Horse Racing Commission’s board of stewards said Saez’s death was “an unfortunate accident.” He was thrown from Montezuma Express while rounding a turn during the eighth race Tuesday.
