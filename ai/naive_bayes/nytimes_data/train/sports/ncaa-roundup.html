http://www.nytimes.com/2014/12/15/sports/ncaabasketball/ncaa-roundup.html 2014-12-15 03:00:50 Tennessee Buries Butler in Second Half Josh Richardson scored 18 of his 20 points in the second half as Tennessee upended No. 15 Butler. === Josh Richardson scored 18 of his 20 points in the second half, and Kevin Punter added 18 as host Tennessee upended No. 15 Butler, 67-55, on Sunday. Butler (8-2) led, 37-25, when Kellen Dunham made a 3-pointer in the opening minute of the second half, but Richardson scored the game’s next 8 points to start a 12-0 run that tied the score. The Bulldogs struggled after that, and Tennessee (4-3) outscored Butler, 42-21, in the second half. Butler shot 6 of 23 from the field and had nine turnovers after halftime. Dunham scored 16 points for Butler, and Kelan Martin added 13. VILLANOVA 85, TEMPLE 62 The Wildcats are 10-0 for the second straight year.
