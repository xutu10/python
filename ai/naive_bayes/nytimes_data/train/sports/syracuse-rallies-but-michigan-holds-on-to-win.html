http://www.nytimes.com/2014/12/03/sports/syracuse-rallies-but-michigan-holds-on-to-win.html 2014-12-03 06:46:08 Syracuse Rallies, but Michigan Holds On to Win Spike Albrecht rattled in a 3-pointer with 31 seconds left and the 17th-ranked Wolverines held on to beat visiting Syracuse in the A.C.C.-Big Ten Challenge. === Spike Albrecht rattled in a 3-pointer with 31 seconds left to put Michigan ahead, and the 17th-ranked Wolverines held on to beat visiting Syracuse, 68-65, on Tuesday night in the A.C.C.-Big Ten Challenge. Albrecht’s shot broke a 63-63 tie. Kaleb Joseph of Syracuse, which had rallied from a 10-point second-half deficit, came up short on a 3-point attempt that would have tied the game at the buzzer.
