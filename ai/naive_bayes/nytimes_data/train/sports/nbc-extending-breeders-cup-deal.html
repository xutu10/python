http://www.nytimes.com/2014/10/30/sports/nbc-extending-breeders-cup-deal.html 2014-10-30 04:03:44 NBC Extending Breeders’ Cup Deal The Breeders’ Cup will stay on NBC and NBCSN through 2025, the NBC Sports Group was to announce Thursday. === The NBC Sports Group will announce Thursday that it is extending its Breeders’ Cup contract for 10 years, ensuring that the races will stay on NBC and NBCSN through 2025. NBC is not paying the Breeders’ Cup for the rights; instead, each side will keep money from the commercials and sponsorships it sells. The contract continues the trend toward long deals in sports. The current four-year deal between NBC and the Breeders’ Cup ends in 2015. This year’s two-day Breeders’ Cup is to start Friday at Santa Anita Park in Arcadia, Calif. The highlight is expected to be the participation of California Chrome, who won the NBCSN’s race coverage is set to be from 5 p.m. to 8 p.m. Eastern on Friday; on Saturday, its 3:30 to 8 p.m. coverage leads into racing on NBC from 8 to 9 p.m. This year, NBC and NBCSN added a series of qualifying races called the Breeders’ Cup Challenge, which has let the company promote the culminating Breeders’ Cup.
