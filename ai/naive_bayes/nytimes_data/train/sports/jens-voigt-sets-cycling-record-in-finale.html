http://www.nytimes.com/2014/09/19/sports/jens-voigt-sets-cycling-record-in-finale.html 2014-09-19 06:10:52 Rider Sets Record in Finale Voigt set a new mark in cycling’s historic Hour Record, racing 31.7 miles around a velodrome in Grenchen, Switzerland. === Jens Voigt set a new mark in cycling’s historic Hour Record, racing 31.7 miles around a velodrome in Grenchen, Switzerland. Ending his career with the record attempt, Voigt, 43, beat the 30.8-mile mark of Ondrej Sosenka in 2005. The Hour Record was previously held by Fausto Coppi, Jacques Anquetil and Eddy Merckx.
